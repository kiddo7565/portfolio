About Me

I am a community taught software engineer, based in Northeast Ohio, working as a freelance software developer. When I am not coding, I can be found practicing/teaching different styles of yoga, reading books, listening to podcasts, and trying to keep my houseplants alive. I love to travel, trying new foods and relaxing in a park on warm sunny days.

What motivates me to create with code?

I am passionate aboout learning, creating and helping people. Coding allows me to combine all three. This passion pushes me forward. While working in diverse industries, I saw firsthand how advancements in technology can do so much good in the world. I want to take part in that positive change. I devote my time to building the products that spark change, community, well-being, and safe spaces.

My Tech Skills

I am most comfortable working with the MERN JavaScript stack, however, I make it a priority to keep up with industry best practices and current and upcoming industry tools. I am more than happy to add a new dev tool to my belt!
